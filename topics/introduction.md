# Manual Technical Management (Dime.Scheduler)
Adds graphical planning capabilities based on Dime.Scheduler to Technical Management for service sales orders

## Introduction
In the Technical Management service module, sales orders for services can be planned based on predefined planning norms per item. This predefined planning norm is transferred to the sales order line, as sales plan. lines, once the item is selected on the sales quote and/or order. From this moment on, planning can take place. Once a Starting Date Time and Resource is defined on a sales plan. line, the line is considered planned.

To make the planning process more intuitive, the app Technical Management (Dime.Scheduler) adds the graphical planner [Dime.Scheduler](https://www.dimescheduler.com/) as the user interface for the sales planning process. 

Bluace also supports planning projects with Dime.Scheduler. This is handled by the app [Extended Time Sheets (Dime.Scheduler)](https://marketplace.microsoft.com/pl-pl/product/saas/pubid.bluacebv1606380518217%7Caid.bluace_extended_timesheets_dime%7Cpappid.64113448-3dd8-47d8-b141-541dc63bc829), which is also available on AppSource.
